The Experience

Where Cuisine
Meets Canvas

Taste of Art is a premier culinary arts event series where renowned chefs and visual artists collaborate to create immersive dining environments. Each evening is a live canvas — curated menus, original installations, and performances that blur the line between a meal and an exhibition.

What To Expect

Four Pillars of
the TASTE OF ART Experience

🍽️
Culinary Theatre
Multi-course tasting menus crafted by featured chefs, each dish paired with the corresponding artwork and narrative.
🎨
Live Art Installations
Original works created in real-time during the event — paintings, sculpture, and mixed media responding to the evening's culinary story.
🥂

Sommelier-selected wine and cocktail pairings that extend the sensory experience beyond the plate.
✨
Collector Access

Signature Moments

The Moments
You'll Remember

01
The Opening Tableau
Guests arrive to a transformed space — the room itself is the first act, every detail a cue for what's ahead.
02
The Chef Introduction
The featured chef walks the room, explaining the evening's concept and the artist whose work inspired the menu.
03
The Live Creation
Mid-dinner, the artist begins their piece. The room watches creation happen in real time alongside the meal.
04
The Reveal
The final course is served as the artwork is completed. A moment of silence, then the room reacts.
05
The Conversation
Post-dinner salon — chef, artist, and guests in open dialogue about process, inspiration, and the intersection of their crafts.
06
The Acquisition
For those moved to own a piece of the evening, a private acquisition process is available before departure.
